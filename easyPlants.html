Easy Indoor Plants

Lavender
A tall purple flowering plant - needs sun and infrequent watering
Snake Plant
A low fleshy plant with dark green and yellow leaves - good with low light and dry air
Spider Plant
Green and white plants with long reaching leaves - just add sunlight and water
Peace Lily
A dark green oval leaved plant with sumularly shaped pink, white, or purple flowers - low light, moderate temperatures, moist soil
Heart leaf Philodendron
A bright green plant with large heart shaped leaves - need low/indirect light and infrequent watering.
Pothos
A plant with small heart shaped leaves of varying colors - good for darker rooms
Various Herbs
Basil, rosemery, oregano, etc. They serve a practical and aestetic purpose at the same time - place in direct sun and water every other day
Asparagus Fern
A bright green plant with long fluffy leaves - adapts to both sun and dim light
Chinese Money Plant
A short plant with circular bright green leaves - likes shade and weekly watering

More of these plants can be found in the resources on the home page - as well as where to buy them
